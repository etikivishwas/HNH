About Us

HNH International Distributions stands as a prominent leader in the distribution landscape, catering to the diverse needs of Uganda and its neighboring countries. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a reliable and innovative player, ensuring seamless access to a wide range of products across various industries.

Vision

HNH International Distributions Ltd is a dynamic and rapidly growing company based in Kampala, Uganda. Established in 2023, we have quickly emerged as a leading player in the distribution landscape of Uganda and neighboring East African countries. Specializing in the efficient supply of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) and other high-quality products, we cater to a diverse range of clients, including retailers, wholesalers, and businesses across East Africa. Our mission is simple: to be the preferred distribution partner in the region, delivering exceptional FMCG goods and a wide variety of other products that meet the ever-evolving needs of our customers. We are committed to providing reliable, timely, and cost-effective distribution solutions that enhance business operations and contribute to the growth of our partners

At HNH International Distributions, we believe in forging long-lasting relationships built on trust and excellence. With a focus on quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction, we strive to be a key contributor to the growth and prosperity of the East African business landscape. As we expand our reach, we continue to innovate and adapt to the changing demands of the market, ensuring our customers always have access to the best products and services available.
